Huawei: we should continue to increase investment in the automobile industry, and the investment in R & D will reach 1 billion US dollars this year

The new product launch of Huawei intelligent car solution 2021 opened at Shanghai auto show today, presenting Huawei's new ideas, new products and new technologies in the transformation and upgrading of the automobile industry, bringing users a new experience in driving intelligent cars.
In addition, Huawei also announced that Huawei has established extensive cooperation with global mainstream car companies, including the joint development of more than 200 industrial partners, and launched five new products, such as harmony intelligent car, covering intelligent cockpit, computing platform, radar, open platform for automatic driving and heat management system.
Wang Jun, President of Huawei intelligent vehicle solutions Bu, said at the meeting: Huawei will continue to increase its investment in the automotive industry. This year, its investment in R &amp; D will reach 1 billion US dollars, with more than 5000 R &amp; D teams, including more than 2000 Automatic drivers.
