AMD: Ryzen 5000 G series processors coming out in a few weeks are perfect for gaming

AMD China announced that the Ryzen 5000G series desktop processor with Radeon graphics is now available and will bring a game-friendly processor to OEM's new desktop system in the next few weeks.
The AMD Ryzen 5000G family of desktop processors with Radeon graphics cards offer a unique combination of features that enable gamers and content creators to accomplish more at an unimaginable speed.
Ryzen 5000G series desktop APU includes three mainstream products, R75700G, R55600G and R35300G, with 8 cores, 6 cores and 4 cores respectively. Compared with the previous generation, this series of APU has been upgraded to Zen3 architecture, but the core display is still the VEGA core display of GCN architecture, and the previous generation has not improved much.
