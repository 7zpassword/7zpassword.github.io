HUAWEI Xu Zhijun: strengthen software in the future, reduce dependence on chips, and increase investment in auto driving parts / software.

Huawei global analysts conference was held on April 12. Xu Zhijun, vice chairman and rotating chairman of Huawei, explained Huawei's overall strategy with the theme of "flying through the clouds is still calm". Huawei's performance in 2020 is in line with expectations, he said. Mobile phone business declined last year, but further improved the layout of notebook, tablet and other hardware areas, so the company's overall revenue kept growing. Facing sustainable development in the future, Huawei has further increased R & D investment, which accounted for 15.9% of sales revenue last year. 2021 is a challenging year for Huawei and the beginning of its future development strategy.
HUAWEI Xu Zhijun: strengthen software in the future, reduce dependence on chips, and increase investment in auto driving parts / software.
When talking about Huawei's future strategy, Xu Zhijun said: first of all, Huawei needs to build the resilience of the industry, so Huawei is optimizing the industrial mix, first of all, strengthening software, further improving software, reducing dependence on chips, and increasing competitiveness. Improve software update capability and find investment opportunities in software field, so as to improve Huawei's service capability. For example, we should increase the development of the cloud field, which is mainly because Huawei believes that software is the root of the cloud; secondly, we should creatively increase the investment in other fields, such as the optical field other than communication; thirdly, we should continue to increase the investment in intelligent driving software, so as to promote the intellectualization and electrification of the automobile industry. HUAWEI will increase investment in auto driving parts.
It home learned that Xu Zhijun, Huawei's chairman in office, mentioned that Huawei will increase its investment in autonomous driving, promote the intellectualization and networking of the automobile industry, and bring Huawei long-term strategic opportunities. The most disruptive industry in the future will also be the automobile industry. Xu Zhijun once again stressed that Huawei's goal is to help car companies build good cars, and also to help car companies sell good cars in the future.
