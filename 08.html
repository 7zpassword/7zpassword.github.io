BYD Han EV won 2021 if design award: the first award-winning car of Chinese brand

BYD announced that Han EV won the 2021 German if design award, becoming the first award-winning car model of Chinese brand.
The international design award jury highly commented: "BYD Han EV provides healthy, safe and environmentally friendly daily commuting and home travel solutions for Chinese users," officials said. The Chinese EV has drawn inspiration from the traditional Chinese culture. Its fashionable and avant-garde appearance and luxurious interior design reinterpret the unique traditional aesthetics of China. Meanwhile, the excellent performance of the Han EV 100 km acceleration of 3.9s and the intelligent network system make the driving experience more exciting. "
Last year, BYD's "global super safe intelligent new energy flagship car" officially launched in Han, including Han EV and Han DM. After the national comprehensive subsidy, the luxury version of the Han EV super long endurance version costs RMB 2298000, the high-performance version of Han EV is 255800 yuan, the flagship version of the Han EV high-performance version is 279500 yuan, and the luxury version of the Han DM four-wheel drive is RMB 219800. The pure electric driving mileage of luxury and Han EV super long-term noble NEDC is 605 km under comprehensive working conditions; the flagship type of Han EV four drive high performance version accelerates 3.9 seconds, which is the fastest mass production new energy car in China; the luxury 100 km plus speed of the HDM four drive performance version is 4.7 seconds, which is the fastest plug-in hybrid car in China.
