JD Technology fully supports digital RMB, pilot payroll and enterprise payments

Digital RMB can be consumed in the entire process of Jingdong's unmanned supermarket. In addition, JD Technology debuted its pilot application and digital solution for using digital yuan for employee compensation and B2B corporate payments for supply chain partners.

Peng Fei, head of the digital RMB project of JD Technology Group, said that the digital RMB can better adapt to the needs of ordinary people in the field of cash. Centring on the digital RMB pilot, JD Technology will provide more professional services for enterprises, merchants and financial institutions to access the digital RMB system.
