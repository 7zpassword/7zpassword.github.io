Huawei Harmony OS Developer Day was held in Shanghai on April 17

Harmony OS Developer Day will be held in Shanghai on April 17. Harmonyos Developer Day is going to present you with a fun developer feast with hard core technology! Here are the latest development of harmonyos, innovation cases and distributed demo experience.
Huawei has announced the agenda for the development of harmonos, including the operating system lecture in the era of the Internet of things, the overview of the development examples of harmonos codelabs, the design and development analysis of the meta service of harmonos, the development platform and tools of harmonos, how to use js / Java to develop the harmonos UI, the use practice of the component library of harmonos, and the distributed development examples of harmonos Multi device, Huawei distributed calendar application development practice, senior developers innovation sharing and so on.
On April 12, Huawei's vice chairman and rotating chairman explained Huawei's overall strategy. He said that in 2021, Huawei will continue to build full scene experience and improve its hardware and software service capabilities. In terms of Harmony system, it is estimated that 100 million devices will experience Harmony operating system in 2021.
At present, harmonyos has attracted more than 20 hardware manufacturers and 280 application manufacturers to participate in the ecological construction. The next step is to launch Harmony operating system on mobile phones.
Wang Chenglu, President of Huawei's consumer business software department and head of AI and wisdom full scene business department, said that Harmony system is a unified language for different devices. Harmony system is a new generation of intelligent terminal operating system, which provides a unified language for the intelligence, interconnection and cooperation of different devices. This year, the conservative target for the equipment equipped with Harmony system is 300 million.
At the previous Huawei matex2 mobile phone conference, Huawei's managing director and CEO of consumer business said: "starting from April this year, Huawei's flagship mobile phones can be upgraded to Harmony OS in succession, and Huawei's matex2 folding screen will be upgraded in the first batch." According to the latest news, Huawei mobile phone users can upgrade to Harmony OS system before June this year, and it is the upgrade of many models.
