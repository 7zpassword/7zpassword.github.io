Dajiang air 2S real photo exposure: 1 inch camera, 5.2k video shooting

Dajiang air2s UAV has passed the FCC certification of the United States, confirming that it will carry the same 11.55v3500mah battery as the previous generation.
The UAV will support 5.4k30p/4k60p/fhd120p video shooting, with a maximum image transmission distance of 12 km and a 31 minute endurance, slightly lower than the 34 minute endurance of the previous generation.
Compared with the previous generation of mavic air2, Dajiang UAV has two new vision sensors on the front, which is more conducive to obstacle avoidance. In addition, the camera of the UAV camera will be equipped with a 1-inch CMOS sensor, which is much larger than the 1 / 2-inch sensor of the previous generation.
Mavic air2, the last generation UAV in Dajiang, uses four in one CMOS sensor with 48 million pixels. The pixel size of the air2s camera is unclear, but it can be expected that the bottom CMOS will bring better image quality. Foreign media predict that the new Dajiang air2s UAV is expected to be released around April 15.
