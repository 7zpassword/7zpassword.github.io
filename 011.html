Huawei launched the intelligent driving computing platform MDC 810, which is twice as powerful as Tesla

 

Huawei's 2021 new product launch of smart car solutions opened at Shanghai auto show today, presenting Huawei's new products and technologies in the transformation and upgrading of the automotive industry.
Huawei said today that it will launch five hi new products, including intelligent cockpit, computing platform, radar, autopilot open platform and thermal management system, such as Hongmeng OS intelligent cockpit, intelligent driving computing platform mdc810, 4D imaging radar, "Huawei Octopus" autopilot open platform and intelligent thermal management system.
At the meeting, Huawei officially released mdc810 intelligent driving computing platform, with computing power up to 400 + tops, which can meet the application scenarios of high-level automatic driving passenger cars and robotaxi. It is the largest intelligent driving computing platform in mass production.
At present, the computing platform has completed all the tests and mass production.
As a comparison, the hw3.0 self-developed chip currently used by Tesla is a computing platform specially built for FSD, which can achieve image processing performance of 2300 frames per second, with a maximum of 144 tops; while NVIDIA's previously released drive Atlan has 1000 tops computing power, which will provide samples to automobile manufacturers and developers as soon as 2023, and is expected to provide samples until 2024 and 2025 It will only be carried by mass production vehicles in 2010.
