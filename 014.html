Huawei disclosed patents related to "In-car Voice Interaction Method" to protect privacy from disclosure

Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. discloses the patent of "An In-car Voice Interactive Method and Equipment", the disclosure number is CN112673423A.

Huawei disclosed patents related to "In-car Voice Interaction Method" to protect privacy from disclosure

According to the patent summary, the in-car voice interaction method and device are designed to protect privacy, including:

Acquire user voice information;

Determine user instruction according to user voice information;

Judge whether the response content for user instruction involves privacy according to user instruction;

According to whether the response content involves privacy, it determines whether to output the response content through privacy protection mode.
