Gemini, a large cryptocurrency exchange platform in the US, now allows users to buy cryptocurrencies such as bitcoin through Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Gemini users can now buy passwords on the platform with their Apple Pay or Google Pay. Users can withdraw money within an hour of making a purchase, the company noted.
The feature supports more than 30 cryptocurrencies, with a Gemini spokesperson pointing out that "all tokens except DAI can be purchased through Apple Pay and Google Pay."
The consolidation of the cryptocurrency industry with traditional financial payment services has been expanding in recent months. In February, crypto payment provider BitPay announced a major integration plan with Apple Pay, allowing its users to connect to Apple Wallet and use cryptocurrencies to Pay for goods and services such as BTC, ETH and LTC.
In March, crypto payments company Simplex also allowed Apple Pay users to buy the coTi cryptocurrency.
